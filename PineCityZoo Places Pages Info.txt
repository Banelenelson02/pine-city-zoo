INSECT HOUSE
<p>The Insect House is home to one of the largest and most diverse insect collections in the world. Here you will see creepy crawlies from all over the world, in every shape and size.</p>

<p>WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?</p>

<p>Insect House is at the eastern side of the zoo, next to the Monkey Trail.</p>


MONKEY TRAIL
<p>Whether you choose to walk or ride, you will find the monkey trail very challenging. Take the 30 minute or 1 hour trail.</p>

<p>Be prepared to get dirty!</p>

<p>WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?</p>

<p>Monkey Trail is at the eastern side of the zoo.</p>


WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

<p>WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?</p>

THE LOST FOREST

<p>WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?</p>
